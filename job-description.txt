Senior Product Designer - Enterprise UX

We are looking for a Senior Product Designer to join our Enterprise Tools team. You will be responsible for designing complex internal applications that help our employees work more efficiently.

Responsibilities:
- Lead design discovery and strategy for enterprise applications.
- Conduct user research and usability testing with internal stakeholders.
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and high-fidelity designs.
- Collaborate with product managers and engineers to define requirements.
- Advocate for accessibility and inclusive design practices.

Requirements:
- 5+ years of experience in product design or UX design.
- Experience with complex enterprise applications or SaaS products.
- Strong proficiency in Figma and prototyping tools.
- Experience with design systems and accessibility standards (WCAG).
- Ability to facilitate workshops and align stakeholders.
- Background in financial services or regulated industries is a plus.
